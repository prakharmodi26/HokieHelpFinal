# Industry (CS|Source)
### **Connect to the source of tech talent. Build your pipeline.**
Software runs on **source code**. The tech workforce runs on **people**.
**CS|Source** , the Virginia Tech Department of Computer Science **industry partner program** , connects companies with the next generation of **software engineers, AI specialists, data scientists, and tech innovators**. It’s where employers discover emerging talent, and where students and alumni connect with companies building the future of computing.
Through CS|Source, organizations can engage directly with **Virginia Tech computer science students and graduates** through recruiting, mentorship, events, and collaborative opportunities designed to build a stronger tech talent pipeline.
Choose the level of partnership that fits your organization, and work with us to help develop the **technology leaders shaping tomorrow’s software, AI, and computing breakthroughs**.
Our industry partnership program benefits employers and students with a range of opportunities.
Explore the benefits of industry partnership with CS|Source. Choose the level that best suits your organization.
