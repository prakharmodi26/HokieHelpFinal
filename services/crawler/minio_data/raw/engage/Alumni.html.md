## Hokies give back
### Student support
  * **[The Anne& George Gorsline Endowed Scholarship](https://giving.adv.vt.edu/gift?=&fund=821082&desc=Anne%20and%20George%20Gorsline%20Endowed%20Sch.%20in%20Computer%20Science)** was established to recognize and promote the continuation of Gorsline's achievement of having females represent 30 to 40 percent of the department's student population during his tenure. Specify the "Anne & George Gorsline Endowed Scholarship in Computer Science" - Fund Number 885589.
  * **[The Barbara Ryder Excellence Fund](https://giving.adv.vt.edu/gift?=&fund=821082&desc=Barbara%20Ryder%25%20%2020Fund%20for%20Excellence%20in%20Computer%20Science)** , which is focused on supporting diversity and inclusion in computer science.
  * **[The George Gorsline Memorial Scholarship](https://giving.adv.vt.edu/gift?=&fund=821082&desc=George%20Gorsline%20Memorial%20Endowment)** is given each year to a rising senior who has made the most significant rebound from his/her freshman year. Specify the "George Gorsline Memorial Scholarship Award" - Fund Number 883979.
  * **[Unrestricted gifts](https://giving.adv.vt.edu/gift?=&fund=881337)** have immediate and flexible impact on students, as well as the department's growth and excellence.
  * **[Endowed scholarships](https://giving.adv.vt.edu/gift?=&fund=821082&desc=Computer%20Science%20Investment%20in%20Excellence%20Scholarship)** help grow our undergraduate scholarship endowment, donate to the CS Investment in Excellence Endowment.
