# Computer science goes big at 2024 Dean's awards celebration
Computer science faculty and staff received nearly 30 percent of this year's Dean's awards.
Chris North (back left), department head Cal Ribbens, Kurt Luther, and Ed Fox. Debbie Zier (left front), Tijay Chung, Lifu Huang, Kirk Cameron, and Matt Hicks. Photo by Peter Means for Virginia Tech.
Nine out of 33 College of Engineering Dean's awards recipients this year were faculty or staff in the [Department of Computer Science](http://cs.vt.edu).
Each spring, the office of [Dean Julie Ross](https://eng.vt.edu/about/leadership-and-directors/vtdeanross.html) recognizes [College of Engineering](http://eng.vt.edu/) faculty and staff whose work promotes education and student success.
